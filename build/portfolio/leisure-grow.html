CGI Imagery,
Brochure &
Packaging

BRIEF
Following the success of Leisure Grow’s Noma Christmas catalogue we were approached by the outdoor living brand to launch its new range of premium outdoor furniture. Our brief was to convey the quality and craftsmanship of each item to a key trade audience.
CLIENT

Leisure Grow Outdoor

Project includes: CGI, Design

SOLUTION
We quickly identified CGI technology as the best way to capture the collection in aspirational exotic locations throughout the world. Our award winning CGI artists developed pioneering new technology to re-create complex details including the rattan weave of furniture materials and outdoor foliage to appear in a challenging high resolution print format.
PACKAGING
The refreshed packaging and imagery was produced alongside a stunning A3 print trade catalogue, and launched at the Summer Outdoor Living Exhibition 2015.

RESULTS

The new imagery and packaging was a resounding success with both Leisure Grow and their customers.

“This is our first foray into CGI, and we’re impressed with the incredibly detailed images which really do justice to the quality of each furniture piece and every detail of its design. The end product gave us the tools we needed to introduce our latest LG Outdoor collection to a key trade audience in a visually engaging and aspirational way.”

- Jodie Grimmer

Managing Director at Leisure Grow
