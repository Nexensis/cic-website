Branding, Packaging
& CGI Imagery

BRIEF
During 2015 we partnered with top electrical hair appliance brand Carmen to launch its new range with British television personality and beauty entrepreneur, Samantha Faiers.
CLIENT

Carmen

Project includes: Design, CGI, Video, Photography

SOLUTION
As lead creative agency, we played a vital role in the launch of the range over the busy festive season with an end-to-end marketing campaign. This encompassed new branding, lifestyle photography, a website creative and 30-second promotional teaser video, which reached audiences through national and regional exposure across the UK.

RESULTS

Utilising our in-depth understanding of trends, colour and materials we not only developed branding, packaging and imagery but also helped to develop the product design. From an initial launch of four products this successful range has now grown to over ten unique lines all imbuing the confident style of Samantha Faiers.

“CIC created a sub brand range which really captures Samantha’s confident, relatable and stylish persona. The quality of the visuals and video content is first class and we’re looking forward to the customer response.”

- Martin Clunn

Marketing Manager at RKW Ltd
