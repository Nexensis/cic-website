Videos, Brochure &
Vehicle Branding

BRIEF
A major tourist attraction in the UK, Warwick Castle is always keen to attract more visitors. To help sell the facilities to the international travel trade we were tasked with creating a series of short promotional videos.
CLIENT

Merlin Entertainments

Project includes: Design, Video

SOLUTION
Showcasing the interior features of the castle we created a series of seven short videos. Each filmed on location, these simple yet stylish videos were then edited with titles in multiple languages offering great versatility for use across the world.

RESULTS

Following the success of these videos we were approached to help broaden the Merlin for Groups marketing suite. A 96 page brochure was produced in conjunction with the launch of their trade site, coach wraps designed and a number of promotion pieces produced for a cross section of Merlin attractions.
