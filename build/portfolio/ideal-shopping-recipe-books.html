Recipe Books

BRIEF
We were commissioned by leading multi-channel home shopping retailer, Ideal Shopping Direct, to produce a series of cookery books exclusively for its UK shopping TV channel and website, Ideal World.
CLIENT

Ideal Shopping Direct Ltd

Project includes: Design, Photography

SOLUTION
We created a collection of A5 hardback books each featuring 40 simple recipes that demonstrate the breadth of meal opportunities to be enjoyed when cooking at home from blending juices to perfecting pressure cooking. Our China-based office handled the printing of tens of thousands of hardback copies, creating an efficient and convenient distribution channel between the client, design teams, printers and factories.

RESULTS

Following the success of the Nutritional Blast recipe book production in early 2015 with over 30,000 books sold, the new additions boosted sales throughout 2015/16.

“We continue to be impressed by the popularity of the first series of cookbooks produced by CIC. Our brief for both projects has been simple – to produce an accessible recipe book for our UK home shopping audience that had a professional look and feel, and CIC does just that.”

- Crystal Pywell

Senior Buyer at Ideal Shopping Direct
