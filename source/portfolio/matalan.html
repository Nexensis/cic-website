Driving in-store sales
up by 40%

BRIEF
To create product imagery and video for the launch of Matalan's new Homestores offering in 3 flagship stores, before the product has been shipped to the UK.
CLIENT

Matalan Homestores

Project includes: CGI, Video

SOLUTION
The products were delivered directly to our studio in Hangzhou, China where they were modelled and textured. Our UK creative teams received the digital files from China and built on-trend roomset layouts to showcase the furniture.
VIDEO
In addition to the imagery, the client requested a new brand/sales video to feature on a large screen placed in the windows of their flagship stores, with the potential of moving the style into social/TV outlets. The video was created entirely from existing assets and motion graphics, making it a convenient and useful extension of the brief and a great result of our multi-disciplinary approach.

RESULTS

The flagship stores enjoyed a 40% increase in sales taken for the related products, and our images can be seen across the website and retail locations nationwide.
