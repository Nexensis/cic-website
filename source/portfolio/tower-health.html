Packaging, Photography, Video & CGI Imagery

BRIEF
2016 has seen people blend, chop, slice and spiralize their way to five a day. Leading this health revolution were Tower Housewares who tasked us with bringing their vision to life.
CLIENT

Tower Housewares

Project includes: Design, Photography, Video, CGI, Product Design

SOLUTION
As an extension to their already successful range, we launched the Tower ‘Health’ sub brand through fresh, new packaging, stunning food photography and informative videos.

RESULTS

Selling over 100,000 units in the first three months, the Spudnik Spiralizer has been an immediate hit. Building on this success and in addition to the ever growing health range, we have developed a series of special edition releases of this product planned for release later in the year.
