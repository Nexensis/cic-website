Our Approach

At CIC we blend disciplines to offer a fully-integrated marketing strategy and content creation solution that takes your product from concept to market.

Einstein once said:

“Imagination is more important than knowledge.”

We believe that both imagination and knowledge are equally important. Whilst imagination allows us to develop unique solutions, it is our knowledge that informs us what will work. Our understanding of upcoming trends, the latest technological advances and awareness of the client’s needs ensures that every solution – no matter how imaginative – will be perfect for you, your business and your market.

With design, imagery, video and editorial teams all under one roof, we blur the boundaries between disciplines. It is this unique integrated approach that yields such effective results – results that capture the imagination our of clients, and retains their loyalty. With offices in the UK, Europe and China, we are the one stop solution for all your creative needs.

From Concept To Market

Whether you are looking for fresh ideas or for someone to bring yours to life, we can support you with all that’s required to take your product from concept to market. From product design to brand development, imagery to TV, each of our departments work in unison to develop the right solution for you. Housing all of these skills under one roof we can not only offer streamlined efficiency but also save you the third party agency fees you may be used to.

It is our philosophy to keep things simple; imagine everything, complicate nothing and deliver excellence through creativity.
